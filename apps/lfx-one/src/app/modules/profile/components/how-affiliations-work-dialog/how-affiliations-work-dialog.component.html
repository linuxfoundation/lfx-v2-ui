Affiliations connect your contributions to the organization you were working for at the time. They are determined using three data sources:

Verified identities

Your connected accounts (GitHub, GitLab, email) link code commits and activity to your profile.

Work experience

Your employment history tells us which organization you were representing during each time period.

Contribution timestamp overlap

By matching when you contributed with your employment dates, we attribute each contribution to the correct organization.

How it works: The system overlaps your contribution timestamps with your work experience to determine which organization to attribute each contribution to. You can override any affiliation if the system gets it wrong.
