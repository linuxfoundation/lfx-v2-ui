# LFX Self Serve Help Documentation

Guides for [app.lfx.dev](https://app.lfx.dev) — manage your membership, communities, and contributions.

## Product Areas

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| [My Dashboard](./dashboards/) | Overview of your memberships, activity, and quick links across LFX. |
| [My Events](./events/) | Register for and manage events associated with your projects. |
| [My Mailing Lists](./mailing-lists/) | Subscribe to and manage mailing lists for your communities. |
| [My Meetings](./meetings/) | Schedule, join, and review meetings for your working groups. |
| [My Groups](./committees/) | View and manage committees and working groups you belong to. |
| [My Documents](./documents/) | Access governance documents associated with your projects. |
| [My Surveys](./surveys/) | Complete and review surveys from your projects and working groups. |
| [My Votes](./votes/) | Participate in elections and votes for your governing boards. |
| [My Badges](./badges/) | View and share digital badges you have earned through LFX. |
| [Profile](./profile/) | Update your name, photo, affiliations, and identity connections. |
| [Account Settings](./settings/) | Manage your email, password, and notification preferences. |
| [Training & Certifications](./trainings/) | Track your Linux Foundation training enrollments and certifications. |
| [My Transactions](./transactions/) | View your payment history and receipts for LFX purchases. |

## Need Help?

If you can't find what you need, [open a support ticket](https://jira.linuxfoundation.org/servicedesk/customer/portal/4).
