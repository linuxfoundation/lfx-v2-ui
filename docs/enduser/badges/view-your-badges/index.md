Your LFX badges are digital credentials that recognize your contributions and achievements across Linux Foundation projects and programs. You can view all your earned badges in one place.

## Steps

1. Sign in to [app.lfx.dev](https://app.lfx.dev).
2. Select **Badges** from the left navigation sidebar.
3. Your earned badges appear in the main content area. Use the tabs to filter by category: **All**, **Certifications**, **Learning**, **Memberships**, **Speaking**, **Event Participation**, **Contributors**, **Program Committee**.
4. Select a badge to see its details, including the issuing project and the criteria met.

## What the badges page shows

- All badges earned across your LFX activity
- The project or program that issued each badge
- Select a badge to view its details, including issue information and any sharing or export options available for that badge type.

## Empty state

If you have not earned any badges yet, the page displays a message indicating no badges are available. Badges are awarded automatically when you meet the qualifying criteria for a program or certification.
