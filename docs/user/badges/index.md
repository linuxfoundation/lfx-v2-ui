The Badges section shows all the LFX credentialing badges you have earned through your participation in Linux Foundation projects and programs. Badges are digital credentials that recognize your contributions, certifications, and involvement across the LFX platform.

## What you can do

- View all badges earned across your projects
- See which project or program issued each badge
- Access sharing and export options from the badge detail view

## Who this applies to

All authenticated users can view their own badges. The badges displayed reflect activity across all projects you participate in.

## Getting started

Go to **app.lfx.dev** and select **Badges** from the left navigation. Your earned badges appear in the main content area. Filter by category using the tabs: **All**, **Certifications**, **Learning**, **Memberships**, **Speaking**, **Event Participation**, **Contributors**, **Program Committee**.
