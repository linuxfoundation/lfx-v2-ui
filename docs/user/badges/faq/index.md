# Badges FAQ

## How do I earn a badge?

Badges are awarded automatically when you meet the qualifying criteria for a Linux Foundation program, certification, or contribution milestone. You do not need to apply manually — the system grants badges based on your recorded activity and certifications.

## Where can I see all my badges?

Go to **Badges** in the left navigation of [app.lfx.dev](https://app.lfx.dev). All badges earned across your projects appear in one list.

## Why don't I see a badge I expected to receive?

There may be a delay between completing a qualifying activity and the badge appearing in your account. If a badge is missing after 24 hours, contact LFX support.

## Are my badges visible to others?

Badges are associated with your LFX profile. Contact LFX support for questions about badge visibility.

## Can I share my badges externally?

Badge sharing and export options vary by badge type. Select a badge to see the options available.

## Do badges expire?

Some badges may have expiry dates. Check the badge detail view for expiry information.

## Who do I contact if a badge is incorrect?

Contact LFX support through the Help Center. Include the badge name and the project it relates to so the support team can investigate.
