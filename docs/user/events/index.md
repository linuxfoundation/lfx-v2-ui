The Events section shows Linux Foundation events relevant to you and your projects. You can browse upcoming events, view event details, and manage your attendance.

## What you can do

- Browse upcoming and past LFX events
- View event details including dates, location, and description
- The Events page shows events you are already registered for. Registration is typically handled on the event's external registration page.
- Filter using the **All Foundations**, **All Roles**, and **All Statuses** dropdowns. Switch between **Upcoming**, **Past**, **Visa Letters**, and **Travel Funding** tabs.

## Who this applies to

All authenticated users can browse events. Event visibility may vary based on your project affiliations and the active lens.

## Navigation

Go to **app.lfx.dev** and select **Events** from the left navigation sidebar. The events page lists upcoming events relevant to your projects and profile.

## Lens context

The events shown may be filtered by your active lens and project context. Switch between the **Me**, **Foundation**, **Project**, and **Org** lenses to see events relevant to each scope.
