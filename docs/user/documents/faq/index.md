# Documents FAQ

## Who can see project documents?

Document visibility depends on your persona. If you cannot see expected documents, contact your project administrator.

## Why don't I see any documents?

Make sure you have the correct project or foundation selected in the lens switcher. Documents are scoped to a specific context. If the correct context is selected and you still see no documents, your project may not have any documents uploaded yet.

## Can I download documents?

Select a document to open its detail view and access available download or view options.

## Can I upload documents?

Document upload availability depends on your project role. Contact your project administrator if you need upload access.

## Who do I contact if a document is missing or incorrect?

Contact your project administrator or LFX support through the Help Center. Include the document name and the project it belongs to.

## Are documents shared across projects?

No, documents are scoped to a specific project or foundation. A document uploaded for one project is not visible to other projects unless explicitly shared.
